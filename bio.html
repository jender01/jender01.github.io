About Me

I was born in Chicago, Illinois, but raised in Madison, Wisconsin, where I
live with my parents, brother, and dog. I come from a family of athletes, and
was raised to be a proficient volleyball player, but instead I chose
my love for the creative arts, particularly drawing and writing, over the
sports career I was intended for. Now I pursue those passions, plus a
newfound passion for computer science, at Tufts University.
